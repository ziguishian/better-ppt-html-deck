# Style Feedback Map

Translate casual feedback into concrete design actions.

## “不够高级”

- Reduce element count.
- Increase whitespace.
- Lower color saturation.
- Unify card radius, border, and shadow.
- Remove cheap gradients and decorative clutter.

## “AI 感太强”

- Reduce blue-purple neon.
- Reduce glow lines and abstract particles.
- Add editorial design, product realism, paper texture, or launch-event restraint.
- Use more real subject cues and fewer generic AI motifs.

## “标题不够冲击”

- Increase title size and weight.
- Reduce subtitle interference.
- Strengthen the visual center.
- Increase title/background contrast.
- Shorten title wording.

## “太像模板”

- Reduce generic card stacks.
- Add topic-specific visual motifs.
- Use a stronger hero visual or composition.
- Vary slide structures across the deck.

## “太花”

- Reduce colors.
- Simplify background.
- Remove decorative elements.
- Strengthen hierarchy and spacing.

## “更像 Apple”

- Increase whitespace.
- Reduce copy.
- Use restrained light or dark backgrounds.
- Strengthen product-grade typography.
- Reduce decorative tech elements.

## “更像科技发布会”

- Use larger titles.
- Use dark background when appropriate.
- Add minimal light/shadow drama.
- Keep a strong visual center.
- Use fewer elements with more tension.
